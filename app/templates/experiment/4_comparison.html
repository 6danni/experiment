Forced Comparison

In this task, you’ll continue working within the same scenario. This time, we’ll present you with two datasets at a time, and you’ll indicate which one you prefer for your modeling process.

This method is known as a forced pairwise comparison. It helps us understand your preferences even when it’s difficult to assign exact prices or ratings to each dataset.

What you’ll do:

  • Review two dataset summaries presented side by side
  • Select the dataset you prefer for improving your model
  • No ties — you must choose one dataset per round

Why this matters:

Your choices will allow us to infer a relative ranking of all datasets based on your preferences. This ranking can help identify which types of data are most valuable to decision-makers like you.
