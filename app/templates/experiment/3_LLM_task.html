Task 2: Language Model Assistance

Task Instructions

In this task, you’ll work with a language model assistant to reason through dataset selection. We want to explore whether the assistant can help you reflect on and communicate your preferences when choosing datasets. The assistant will not make decisions for you — instead, it will help you clarify your goals and consider trade-offs between options.

The assistant will ask you a few open-ended questions to guide your thinking. Your job is to describe your reasoning and preferences in free text — just as you would explain your thought process to a colleague. The language model may follow up with a clarification or suggestion, and you'll have a chance to respond again.

You will not make final decisions here — instead, we’re interested in how you communicate your preferences and whether the model can meaningfully support that process.
